## Introduction
Craftsman Lighting is in high demand throughout the United States and the world.  3D designers that can create and expand on historical designs for manufacturing are highly sought after.

For this design project, you are tasked with the challenge of creating historic architectural lighting fixtures based on the Arts and Crafts Movement.
